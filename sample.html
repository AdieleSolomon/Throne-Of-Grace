Meet Our Global Lead Pastors

The Elevation Church is led by Pastors Godman and Bolarinwa Akinlabi and supported by a global team of Pastors and Ministers. PG is an accomplished author, speaker, leadership expert and a relationship coach. PB is a renowned publisher, finance expert and the convener of Made for More Conference for women’s empowerment.

Godman Akinlabi

Global Lead Pastor

Pastor Godman Akinlabi, as the Global Lead Pastor, is a transformative leader, teacher, and global conference speaker. His commitment to empowering leaders and making a positive impact on societies is evident through initiatives like the Pistis Foundation and events such as the Exponential Conference and Gravity.

Pastor Bola Akinlabi, the Global Co-Lead Pastor, brings over a decade of finance experience and a heart for women’s empowerment. She pioneers Jewels, the Women’s Ministry, and hosts the annual Made for More Conference, equipping women with spiritual and practical tools for success.
